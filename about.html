ABOUT PIC-WILD

My name is Fletcher Zulu. I live in the beautiful African country of Zambia. Over the years, I have visited most of the national parks in Zambia, as well as other African countries. During these visits, I was accompanied by family and friends, and we had the opportunity to take a lot of wildlife pictures.(These pictures were taken in different African countries) I was inspired to build a website for these pictures so that you can see the magnificent creations of God and witness His glory through them.
